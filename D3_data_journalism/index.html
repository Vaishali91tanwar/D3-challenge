Correlations Discovered Between Health Risks and Poverty, Age, Income

In majority of the states people in the age group of 36 to 40 lack healthcare. Also, with the increase in the poverty the health risks increase. Healthcare risks are high in states with average household income lying in the range of 40000 to 50000 USD.

Smoking percentage is high in states with higher percentage of poverty.In states with the average age between 36 to 40 have a high rate of smoking. States having a higher median household income have a lower smoking percentage.

States with higher poverty percentage have higher number of obese people. Higher percentage of obesity has been found in states with age group between 36 and 40. States with lower average household income have greater number of obese population.
